Miles Manley

Chief Executive Officer

Bio

Miles founded Zel at age 15, with the goal of creating a fast, scalable, and decentralized blockchain infrastructure for developers to utilize and develop upon.

Miles, now 17, is still leading and developing various projects within Zel; alongside the support of 37 team members and thousands of community members. Zel it self, is a POW cryptocurrency utilizing the unique ZelHash Algorithm.

Miles spends most of his time on Zel working on various facets of the project. He looks forward to the future that lays ahead, not only in the development of Zel and it's projects, but also in the opportunities it can offer people in the future, from all walks of life.
